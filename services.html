Our Services

Painting Solutions for Every Need

Residential, commercial, interior, exterior — we deliver premium results on every surface, every time.

What We Provide

Complete Painting & Finishing Services

Interior Painting

Walls, ceilings, trim, and doors. We transform every room with precision brushwork, smooth roller finishes, and clean-cut edges that make your home feel brand new.

Exterior Painting

Siding, stucco, fascia, and trim. We use weather-resistant, UV-stable coatings that protect your home and boost curb appeal for years.

Cabinet Refinishing

Give your kitchen or bathroom a high-end makeover at a fraction of replacement cost. Professional spray and brush finishes that look factory-fresh.

Feature Walls & Decorating

Bold accent walls, faux finishes, Venetian plaster, and specialty textures that become the focal point of any room.

Fence & Deck Staining

Restore and protect outdoor wood surfaces with professional-grade stains and sealers that withstand the Bay Area's coastal climate.

Commercial Painting

Offices, retail spaces, and multi-unit buildings. We work around your schedule to minimize disruption and deliver on deadline.

Drywall Repair & Installation

Patch holes, fix water damage, and install new drywall with seamless finishing. We handle the prep so the paint looks perfect.

Pressure Washing

Deep clean driveways, walkways, decks, and siding before painting or as a standalone service. Restore surfaces to like-new condition.

Wall Texturing

Orange peel, knockdown, skip trowel, and smooth finishes. We match existing textures or create something entirely new.
