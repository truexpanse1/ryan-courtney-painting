Our Story

Built on Craftsmanship, Driven by Results

Ryan Courtney founded R. Courtney Painting with a simple philosophy: treat every home like it's your own. What started as a one-man operation in San Mateo has grown into one of the Bay Area's most respected painting companies — still with Ryan personally overseeing every single project.

We believe the difference between a good paint job and a great one comes down to preparation, premium materials, and an obsession with clean lines. That's why we never cut corners on surface prep, always use top-tier paints and coatings, and do a final walkthrough with every client before we pack up.

  • Owner-operated — Ryan is on every job
  • Premium Benjamin Moore & Sherwin-Williams paints
  • Fully licensed, bonded & insured
  • Fixed-price quotes — no surprises, ever
